About ICAM

Mission

Accelerate adoption of additive manufacturing by delivering practical solutions that improve performance, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness.

What We Do

Co-develop parts and processes with companies, validate materials and designs, and build talent through customised short courses.

Who We Serve

Aerospace, marine, precision engineering, biomedical, electronics, and built environment partners seeking AM-enabled solutions.

Our innovation platforms (click to expand)
  • Design & Simulation: Generative design, topology optimisation, lattices, and process simulation to reduce trial-and-error.
  • Materials & Processes: Polymers (FDM/FFF, SLS), metals (DED, PBF), and composites; test coupons to full parts.
  • Certification & QA: Test plans, NDT, mechanical testing, and documentation to support qualification and audits.
